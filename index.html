Security Through Transparency

We openly publish our business infrastructure setup. Even with this blueprint in hand, outside forces cannot access our records. Here is why our environment remains unhackable:

  • Zero-Trust Source Code (GitHub & Vercel): Our public website is a hollow shell. No passwords, client histories, or security logs are stored within this design layout.
  • Advanced AES-256 Vault Encryption (Supabase Database): All submitted job logs and client data are immediately scrambled using AES-256 math parameters. Raw data looks like unreadable symbols to outside eyes.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) Guards: Automated database locks stop all data requests at the door. If a request does not carry the Admin's unique master identity token, connection is instantly dropped.
  • Isolated Form Routing (Formspark): Client intake applications bypass standard, unsecured email forms. Data maps through direct, encrypted point-to-point streams straight to the Admin.
